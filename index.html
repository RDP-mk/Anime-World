Anime List

Anime is an animation style originating from Japan that has grown into a global cultural phenomenon. While the term "anime" in Japan refers to all animation regardless of origin, outside of Japan it specifically denotes Japanese-produced animation known for its distinct visual style, diverse genres, and complex storytelling.
